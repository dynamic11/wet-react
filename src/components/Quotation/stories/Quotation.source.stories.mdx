# Quotation

Using the `footer` and `cite` props it is possible to add, alter and omit footers (author) and citations

## Omitting Both

If both the `footer` and `cite` attributes are left empty, they will be omitted

<Canvas>
  <Story name="NoSource" args={{ children: 'Yeah, I said it, and you can quote me on that!', reversequote: false, reversesource: false, footer: '', cite: '' }}>
    {QuoteTemplate.bind({})}
  </Story>
</Canvas>

## Omitting Citation

If the `cite` attribute is left empty, it will be omitted

<Canvas>
  <Story name="NoCite" args={{ children: 'Yeah, I said it, and you can quote me on that!', reversequote: false, reversesource: false, footer: 'quote from man quoted', cite: '' }}>
    {QuoteTemplate.bind({})}
  </Story>
</Canvas>

## Omitting Footer

If the `footer` attribute is left empty, it will be omitted

<Canvas>
  <Story name="NoFooter" args={{ children: 'Yeah, I said it, and you can quote me on that!', reversequote: false, reversesource: false, footer: '', cite: 'journal' }}>
    {QuoteTemplate.bind({})}
  </Story>
</Canvas>

## Adding both

If both the `footer` and `cite` attributes are provided, they will both be added

<Canvas>
  <Story name="Source" args={{ children: 'Yeah, I said it, and you can quote me on that!', reversequote: false, reversesource: false, footer: 'quote from man quoted', cite: 'journal' }}>
    {QuoteTemplate.bind({})}
  </Story>
</Canvas>
